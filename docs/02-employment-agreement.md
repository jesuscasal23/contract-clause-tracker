# Employment Agreement

This Employment Agreement is made between **Vertex Robotics Inc.** (the "Company") and **Dr. Elena Fischer** (the "Employee"), effective January 15, 2025.

## 1. Position and Duties

The Employee shall serve as Director of Engineering and shall report directly to the Chief Technology Officer. The Employee shall devote her full business time and attention to the performance of her duties. The Employee agrees to comply with all Company policies in effect from time to time.

## 2. Compensation

The Company shall pay the Employee an annual base salary of $185,000, payable in accordance with the Company's standard payroll practices. The Employee shall be eligible to participate in the Company's annual bonus plan, with a target bonus of 20% of base salary. The Employee shall also be eligible for equity awards as determined by the Board of Directors.

## 3. Non-Compete

During the term of employment and for a period of twelve (12) months following termination, the Employee shall not, directly or indirectly, engage in or provide services to any business that competes with the Company within the field of autonomous robotics. This restriction applies within any geographic territory in which the Company conducts business. The Employee acknowledges that this restriction is reasonable and necessary to protect the Company's legitimate business interests.

## 4. Non-Solicitation

For a period of twelve (12) months following termination, the Employee shall not solicit any employee of the Company to leave their employment. The Employee shall also not solicit any customer of the Company for the purpose of providing competing services.

## 5. Intellectual Property

All inventions, discoveries, and works of authorship created by the Employee in the course of employment shall be the sole property of the Company. The Employee hereby assigns all right, title, and interest in such works to the Company. The Employee shall promptly disclose any such inventions to the Company.

## 6. Termination

The Company may terminate the Employee's employment at any time, with or without cause. The Employee may resign upon thirty (30) days written notice to the Company. Upon termination, the Employee shall return all Company property, including laptops, documents, and access credentials.

## 7. At-Will Employment

Notwithstanding anything to the contrary, employment with the Company is at-will. Nothing in this Agreement shall be construed to guarantee employment for any specific duration.
